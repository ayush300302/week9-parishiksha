# Prompt Comparison

## Permissive Prompt Results

**Query:** What is Newton's first law of motion?
**Answer:** Newton's first law of motion, also known as the law of inertia, states that an object continues to be in a state of rest or of uniform motion along a straight line unless acted upon by an unbalanced force.


**Query:** Why do passengers fall forward when a bus stops?
**Answer:** Passengers fall forward when a bus stops because of the law of inertia. When the bus is moving, the passengers are also in a state of motion. When the bus suddenly stops, the lower part of their body, which is in contact with the seat, comes to rest. However, the upper part of their body tends to continue in the same state of motion because of its inertia, causing them to fall forward.


**Query:** Calculate the gravity on the surface of the Moon.
**Answer:** To calculate the gravity on the surface of the Moon, we can use the formula:

g = G * M / R^2

where:
g = gravity on the surface of the Moon
G = universal gravitation constant (6.67 x 10^-11 N m^2 kg^-2)
M = mass of the Moon (7.35 x 10^22 kg)
R = radius of the Moon (1.74 x 10^6 m)

Plugging in the values, we get:

g = (6.67 x 10^-11 N m^2 kg^-2) * (7.35 x 10^22 kg) / (1.74 x 10^6 m)^2
g = 1.62 m/s^2

So, the gravity on the surface of the Moon is approximately 1.62 m/s^2, which is about 1/6 of the gravity on the surface of the Earth.
